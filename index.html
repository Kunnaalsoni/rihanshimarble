About Us

We at Rihanshi Marble offer a wide range of marble Decors with exclusive designs crafted by hand. Carrying a tradition for centuries, marble is one of the oldest and foremost durable and admired stones. Rihanshi Marble was established with a focus on beautifying people's living areas with this latest fashion that too without taking away from their stylish homes. We offer a wide range of handcrafted products starting from specially crafted Marble furniture tops to marble decoratives like trays, Lamps, Vases, glasses and many other accessories in Natural Stones like White Makrana Marble, Granite Marble, Banswara Marble and many more. We like to think of each of our products as a small masterpiece that is made with utmost care and attention. We have a long history of customer satisfaction, providing our customers quality items while delivering value for money. We have been passionately crafting ornate marble decor since 2014. Our team members, headed by Mr. Shailendra Singh Hada (8 years of Industry Experience) are highly skilled in their respective departments, from sourcing to production and sales, from beginning to end.
